About

This project maps locations from the Marvel Cinematic, Television, and comic Universes in New York.

The Marvel Cinematic Universe features incredible amounts of action in New York, which begs the question: why does anyone live there? Your city has been destroyed by the Avengers, physically twisted by Dr. Strange, and the Staten Island Ferry is sawed in half during a fight between Spider-Man and Adrian Toomes (Vulture).
